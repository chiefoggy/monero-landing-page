Why Use Monero?

Privacy By Design

Monero uses advanced cryptography to ensure that all transactions are untracable and unlinkable.

Financial Freedom

No one can block, censor, or freeze your transactions, offering you true control of your finances.

Fungibility

Monero is one of the few truly fungible cryptocurrencies, meaning each unit of Monero is identical and interchangeable.
This is because no transaction history is tied to its coins, unlike coins like Bitcoin.
